This problem statement looks huge, but it is actually just asking you to build an ERP module for ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance).

Think of it like this:

The company wants one software where they can track how environmentally friendly, socially responsible, and legally compliant they are.

What is ESG?

Imagine you own a company.

People don't only judge you by profit.

They also ask:

🌍 Are you polluting the environment?
👨‍💼 Are your employees treated well?
📜 Are you following company laws and policies?

These three together are called ESG.

In one sentence

EcoSphere is an ERP module that automatically tracks a company's environmental impact (carbon emissions), social responsibility (CSR and employee engagement), and governance (policies, audits, compliance), computes ESG scores for departments and the organization, and encourages participation through gamification with XP, badges, rewards, and leaderboards.
